Babylonian Flood Myth

The gods were displeased with the human race, so they held a council which agrees that human should be drowned.

Ea, the god of wisdom, want to spare human being. Ea told Uta-Napishtim to build a ship for his family and all living creatures.

For six days and nights the rain flooded everything on earth. On the seventh day the rain stop, everything on earth come mud except of Uta-Napishtim family.

Enlil, the god of tempest was angered about the human had been spared, but after Ea’s reconciliation, Enlil gave Uta-Napishtim and his wife immortality.
